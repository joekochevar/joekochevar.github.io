My Role

I'm a System Design Engineer for the BE-4 program. Essentially what that means is that my team configures the top level engine design for each individual engine, and designs all of the connecting parts in the engine: tubes, ducts, secondary structure, ancillary systems, etc. For the BE-4, these parts are approximately 80% of the total engine part count. My daily work in this capacity involves:
  • Detail Design and Drafting
  • BOM Management
  • Structural analysis
  • ​Large Assembly Management
  • Configuration Data Management
